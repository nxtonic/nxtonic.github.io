nectar by hypercode

nectar is a dashboard for monitoring and gathering analytics data for docker containers.

All sorts of web applications are now being supported by some kind of cloud infrastructure, but you need to know that your containers are functioning at their best possible level of performance. Enter nectar, allowing you a greater insight into your containers' efficiency and suggesting where you could improve.

n.vis by hypercode

n.vis is a networked audio visualizer application that takes a sound input and creates frequency-reactive graphics for music. The output can be piped through to other programs, on other machines, and utilises a webview platform interface so it is highly customizable, modular and hackable.

We made this for @attorna to perform his live show with minimal processing overhead while gaining maximum functionality that other programs in the market simply did not possess.
